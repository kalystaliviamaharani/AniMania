WHAT’S ON

AniMania is bursting with exciting events for fans of all ages!

Enjoy panels with voice actors and creators, exclusive anime screenings, and thrilling competitions like cosplay contests and trivia battles. Catch live performances, including idol shows and karaoke, and explore our Exhibition Hall packed with vendors and fan-made treasures. Whether you're here to learn, compete, or just have fun, AniMania offers a full weekend of non-stop anime and pop culture excitement. Don't miss a moment - check the schedule and join the fun!
